CSU Chancellor Mildred Gardia, AI forward initiative (2025)

In 2025, CSU Chancellor Mildred Garcia along with the Office of Governor Gavin Newsom proposed AI leading initiatives to be implemented across all 23 CSU campuses. A private initiative with major technology companies, including Adobe, Google, AWS, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Open AI, and others. This initiative aims to create an AI empowered higher education system by providing more than 460,000 students and 63,000 faculty and staff with access to AI tools such as ChatGPT to enhance e learning, teaching, research, and workforce preparation. Key strategies include the creation of an AI Commons Hub offering tools and training, partnerships to develop innovative AI drive teaching methods, and workforce training opportunities that connect students with AI focused industries. Throughout the AI Workforce Acceleration Board and new faculty led projects, CSU seeks to prepare students with ethical and practical skills while strengthening California’s workforce and positioning the university system as a global leader in responsible AI adoption in higher education
